Artichoke Quiche

Description

Easy quiche recipe to give the impression you are a deadly chef

Ingredients for the dough

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 3 spoons of butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 coffee spoon of yeast

Ingredients for the filling

  • 1 can of artichoke
  • 300g of cottage cheese
  • 1 onion
  • 3 eggs, 200ml cream, 3 spoons grana padano (mix)

Steps

  1. Mix the ingredients for the dough and put it in the fridge
  2. Saute 1 onion well chopped with butter
  3. Add artichoke, cottage cheese and salt
  4. Prepare the mix and add it to the rest of the filling
  5. Add dough to the quiche pan and make sure it is even and thin
  6. Add the filling and bring it to the oven at 200 degrees celsius for 50 minutes
