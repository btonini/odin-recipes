Asparagus Soup

Description

An easy and very tasty soup to make winter a bit cozier

Ingredients

  • 1 Onion
  • Flour
  • 1 Canned White Asparagus
  • Cream

Steps

  1. Grate 1 onion and saute it in a pan
  2. Add 1 spoon of flour and mix it well
  3. Blend half can of asparagus with its water and 50ml of cream. Add the blended mix to the pan and keep stirring
  4. Chopp the other half can of asparagus and add to the pan
