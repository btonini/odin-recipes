Strogonoff

Description

A delicious Brazillian dish that can be made with chicken or beef

Ingredients

  • 1 Onion
  • 1kg Chicken Breast
  • 250g Mushroom
  • Ketchup
  • Worcestershire Sauce
  • Cream
  • Strong Spirit (Rum, Gin, Vodka)

Steps

  1. Chop the chicken breast and bring it to a big pan for around 10 minutes
  2. Grate the onion and saute it with some butter
  3. Remove the water that came out from the chicken and save it in a glass
  4. Add the onion and 1 glass of Gin to the big pan and set it on fire
  5. When the flames are over, add back the chicken's water and fill with hot water to cover the ingredients
  6. Add the mushroom chopped, 2 big spoons of ketchup, around 75ml of Worcestershire sauce and salt
  7. Leave it boiling for around 15 minutes
  8. Add 75ml of cream when the stove is already off
